EMPOWERING AFRICA, UNITING VOICES

"Developing the next generation of African leaders, driving positive change."

Our vision

Developing the African human capital into a global human powerhouse as a set equipped to solve African challenges with a global standard

Our mission

Invest in the development of young African human capital potential into asset through annual incubation programs that encompass training, mentorship and internship with the aid of strategic partnerships, support and sponsorship

Our Impact

Bringing out Sense of Ladership: Tolu's Story

Oyediran Toluwanimi was one of the participants at the Hail Africa Cohort 1 programme. He was in charge of all areas of the virtual aspect of the programme. Touwanimi is a student leader who teaches his peers academically. Toluwanimi was one of the Youth advisory Group 2024 of the Generation Global Platform. He has builded up leadership skills thanks to HAIL Africa.

Empowering Yong Minds: Emmnauel's Journey

Nwigwe Emmanuel, a student from Lagos Progressive Senior Secondary School, discovered his passion for graphics design through our incubation programme. With mentorship and training, Emmanuel developed his skills and gained confidence.Today, Emmanuel is a talented young designer, equipped to make a lasting impact in his community. His story is a testament to the power of empowerment and the potential of Africa's youth.

Unlocking Potential : Surajudeen Ali's Story

Hail Africa Incubation Programme has transformed the life of Surajudeen Ali . Through our programme, Surajudeen discovered his potential skills and developed a passion for graphics design and fashion design. Our mentorship and training have equipped Surajudeen to make a positive impact in his community. His story is a testament to the power of empowerment and the potential of Africa's youth.
