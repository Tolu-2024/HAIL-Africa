About HAIL Africa

This initiative aims to provide a human asset capacity building and leadership development platform called (HAIL AFRICA - HUMAN ASSET INCUBATION LAB) that gives access to, train and mentors young people on personal development ( e.g maximizing self potential, capacity building, problem solving skills…), digital skills, Entrepreneurship, leadership and impact making, global opportunities maximization (grants, fellowships, scholarships etc,). These programs will produce young leaders that will positively influence and leads across the 360⁰ circle of their influence and network in schools, community and different industries contributing to the overall development of the world with the support of the community stakeholders, partners, sponsors, volunteers etc. This initiative is a social enterprise that runs in annual year cohort and upon completion participants will enjoy discount advance services provided by our partners in their area of interest and also have other opportunities provided by the HAIL Africa for participants with an effective MOU.
